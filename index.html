What Are Exosomes?

Exosomes are tiny vesicles naturally released by cells in the body, playing a key role in cell communication. In skincare they are derived from stem cells and used to promote tissue repair, collagen production, and overall rejuvenation. FAQ's

Dermaplaning is a non-invasive exfoliation treatment that uses a sterile blade to remove dead skin cells and fine facial hair (peach fuzz). This process enhances skin smoothness, allows for better absorption of skincare products and creates a flawless base for makeup. It’s especially effective for reducing the appearance of acne scars and dullness.

Exosomes are regenerative cell-derived vesicles that carry growth factors, proteins, and genetic material to promote skin healing and renewal. When used in aesthetic treatments, exosomes can enhance collagen production, reduce inflammation, and improve overall skin texture and elasticity. They’re often added to microneedling or laser sessions for amplified results.

A Jet Peel is a high-tech, needle-free facial that delivers a powerful stream of oxygen and saline into the skin to exfoliate, hydrate, and infuse nutrients at a deep level. It’s ideal for sensitive skin and offers immediate rejuvenation without discomfort or downtime. Compared to traditional facials, Jet Peels reach deeper layers of the skin for more impactful results.
